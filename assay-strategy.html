Panel Tiering Strategy

Risk-adaptive panels.
Built around clinical episodes.

Oriole Sciences structures panel recommendations in four tiers, each calibrated to patient risk state, episode complexity, and reimbursement defensibility — not a one-size-fits-all catalog.

1
Focused
Uncomplicated & Low-Risk

Narrow, justified panel configuration for low-complexity presentations. Every target clinically defensible. Cost-efficient without sacrificing documentation integrity.

2
Enhanced
Recurrent or Elevated-Risk

Broader coverage with documented clinical rationale for recurring episodes or elevated patient risk. Panel depth justified by treatment consequence, not convention.

3
Expanded
Resistance or Treatment Escalation

Configuration built for episodes where resistance patterns or treatment escalation change the clinical path. Depth aligned to decision — and documented accordingly.

4
Comprehensive
Complex or Immunocompromised

Full diagnostic depth for complex presentations and immunocompromised patients. Maximum breadth, fully documented — every target tied to patient complexity and risk state.
