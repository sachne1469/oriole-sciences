About Oriole Sciences

We exist because
the problem is real.

A molecular diagnostics company built around one conviction: the labs and providers who can't afford to get it wrong deserve a partner who sees the whole system.

Our Mission

Intelligence no equipment vendor or billing shop can give you.

Molecular diagnostics is a hundred-moving-part problem. Most operators are left to wire it together alone — selecting equipment from one vendor, billing from another, staffing from a third. None of them see the whole system. None of them own the outcome.

Oriole Sciences was built to be that one partner. We see which workflows hold up, which codes survive an audit, where the margin leaks — and we build or rebuild the entire lab program around what the data says.

Why We Exist
The Problem

Volume without defensibility.

Payers are no longer reimbursing broad molecular programs without documented justification. Labs built around volume — not defensibility — are facing skyrocketing denial rates.

The Gap

Vendors who own a piece, not the outcome.

Equipment vendors, billing companies, and staffing agencies each solve one part — but no one holds the program together. The gaps between vendors are where molecular programs fail.

Our Answer

One partner. Every decision.

We engineer the entire molecular lab program — from build to billing — as one integrated system. Every decision made with the full picture. Every piece accountable to the whole.

Our Approach

Data first. Always.

Every program decision we make — how a lab is sized, how panels are configured, how reimbursement is structured — is grounded in real market data.

Regional prevalence data. Payer behavior patterns. Denial trend analysis. Throughput modeling. Not convention, not sales forecasts — the actual intelligence your market is already showing.

That's what Defensible PCR means in practice: a program built on evidence, validated operationally, and documented to survive scrutiny at every level.

Principle 01

Principle 02

Principle 03

Document as if the auditor is already in the room. Because eventually they are.

Principle 04

Own the outcome, not just a piece of it.
