The Validation Problem

Most labs validate under pressure, not by design.

Without specialized expertise, validation becomes reactive. Generic protocols, incomplete documentation, and last-minute regulatory scrambles extend timelines — and create audit exposure that persists long after the program goes live.

What Reactive Validation Produces
Extended timelines — Incomplete study design forces re-runs, delaying program launch by months
Regulatory gaps — CLIA, COLA, and CAP requirements interpreted inconsistently, creating compliance risk
Documentation failures — Validation records assembled after the fact don't survive real audit scrutiny
Payer exposure — Validation gaps become billing vulnerabilities when payers request documentation to support claims
